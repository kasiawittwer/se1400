Sticker Packs

This project features a collection of themed sticker sheets. Each pack was thoughtfully designed to reflect its unique theme. The book stickers celebrate the joy of reading with cozy visuals and literary quotes. The mental health pack offers uplifting reminders and calming messages designed to support self-care and mindfulness. The hiking sheet is filled with adventurous and nature-inspired graphics, perfect for outdoor enthusiasts.
