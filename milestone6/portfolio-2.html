Deck of Cards

For this project, I designed a deck of cards inspired by the world under the ocean. Each card features unique illustrations of various ocean creatures, from silly jellyfish to graceful dolphins, giving each one its own character and charm. The deck bursts with bright, whimsical colors, adding to the playful theme, and each suit is distinctively colored to create an engaging and visually dynamic experience. This ocean-themed deck transforms the card-playing experience into an undersea adventure, blending fun, art, and imagination into every card.
