Destination Poster

I wanted to showcase the timeless beauty of Greece through a bold, geometric lens. This minimalist, monochromatic poster captures the iconic architecture of Greece in a palette of whites and blues, reminiscent of the sun-washed buildings that dot the landscape. With clean lines and crisp shapes, it evokes the serenity and elegance of Greece's coastal towns, blending tradition with modern design. A striking tribute to Greece's architectural charm.
