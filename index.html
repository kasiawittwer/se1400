Website Summary

What content will the site contain?

The content for this site will contain projects I've created to build my portfolio. These will feature projects I've completed in classes and for work.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

I will use quality images I have taken of my projects as well as mockups of my digital projects.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Users should care about my website because it will serve as a showcase of my skills and creativity. The portfolio should highlight my design ability, so users can see the depth and variety of my skills.

Why should a site be built for this?

Building a website for my portfolio will serve as a polished and porfessional platform to showcase my work. This will make my work accessible to potentail clients and employers, and present my work in a visually appealing way.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I want users to explore my work as I showcase my projects. I want them to learn about me so they get to know my background, skills, and my design approach. I also want to make it easy for users to reach out for collaboration.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal of a portfolio site is to showcase my skills, experience, and creative work in a professional and engaging way. It serves as an online resume and gallery, for potential employers, clients, or collaborators.
